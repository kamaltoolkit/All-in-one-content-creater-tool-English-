📌 About Us

Welcome to the 7th CPC Salary Calculator (Advanced) — a completely free and user-friendly tool designed to help Central Government Employees calculate their salary structure accurately.

This tool is built with the latest 7th Pay Commission rules and includes:

  • Pay Matrix Level & Basic Pay calculation
  • Flexible Dearness Allowance (DA%) input
  • House Rent Allowance (HRA) as per city category (X, Y, Z)
  • Transport Allowance (TA) with DA
  • Common deductions like NPS, CGEGIS, Professional Tax & estimated Income Tax
  • Gross Salary and Net Salary estimation
  • Easy "Load Example" feature for quick testing

The calculator runs entirely in your browser (client-side). No data is saved or sent to any server.

🎯 Our Purpose

We aim to provide a simple, fast, and accurate salary estimation tool so that government employees can better understand their pay structure, especially after every DA hike or pay revision.
